Omelette Recipe

Classic Omelette

Prep

5 mins

Cook

5 mins

Total

10 mins

Ingredients


4 large eggs

room temperature


1/4 cup whole milk


1/4 teaspon kosher salt


2 tablespoons unsalted butter


Instructions


Bear the eggs and milk until light and frothy.


Heat a nonstick skillet over mediu heat. Add the butter.


Pour in the eggs. Let them set for a few seconds.
